The Company

Athabasca Holdings, LLC is a software company based outside Austin, TX in Manor, TX that is focused on leveraging artificial intelligence to solve problems in business and computing.

Leadership

Saint Jovite Youngblood

CEO and founder who decided to expand into software and Artificial Intelligence after seeing how it can become a great equalizer and boost to companies to become more productive and profitable.

He also runs Youngblood Metals Mining, a company that offers a host of collectibles services spanning rare and vintage coins, watches, toys, jewelry, stamps, and precious stones and metalsfrom silver to palladium. They take pride in handling all inquiries, with the philosophy that no collection is too large or too small. Acting as a purchaser for a network, they make every effort to ensure customer satisfaction.

Before starting Youngblood Metals Mining he worked as the general manager of Miracle Mile Coin in San Marino and oversaw a family-owned business that focused primarily on estate collections, vintage toys, and precious metals. Where he guided retail and wholesale activities, and ensured efficient allocation of resources.
